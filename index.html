About Us

Marine Electric is a company specialized in electrical installations and marine electronics with over 25 years of experience in the sector.

Our team of highly qualified engineers and technicians is committed to providing top quality and safety solutions for all types of vessels, from small sailboats to large yachts.

We work with the best materials and equipment on the market, guaranteeing maximum reliability and durability of our installations.
